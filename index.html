Radical Engineers

Radical people for radical projects



Basic idea

We build teams that bundle the relevant skills (e.g. designer, front-end developer, data engineer) and pair them with radical organisations.

This can be demand-driven, where organisations reach out to the collective with a specific problem, or a delegation visits the organisation to assess what technologies and infrastructures may help them be more effective and educate them on what is possible.

What we do

  1. We have a strict onboarding program that helps engineers understand the intricacies of radical organisations, including financial constraints, building things that can be maintained, being realistic about the scope of the project (given how long someone is willing to work on a projects), etc.
  2. We host workshops where our engineers connect with radical organisations and spend time gaining a deep understanding of their problems (either technical or non-technical).
  3. We connect with organisations like Critical Incubator to source time-worthy projects.
